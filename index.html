Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our math tutoring program and the enrollment process.

We specialize in primary school math tutoring, focusing specifically on children in Years 3 to 6. This is the optimal window to build mathematical cognitive pathways, prepare for scholarship testing, and ready students for the selective high schools exams (typically sat in Year 6).

We offer small group classes (maximum of 8 students) to ensure a perfect balance of collaborative peer learning and customized individual guidance. Classes are run weekly, incorporating conceptual teaching, speed drills, reasoning challenges, and regular diagnostic testing.

We offer both! You can select in-person sessions at our modern learning hubs or attend our fully interactive digital classrooms. Our online setup uses digital write-on tablets and screen sharing, offering the same level of micro-feedback and high-impact engagement as our physical classes.

Selective school entrance tests are designed to evaluate *aptitude* and *cognitive logical ability*, not just rote memory of school formulas. Standard school math covers simple operations. We focus heavily on spatial logic, logical syllogisms, pattern analysis, and numerical problem solving, teaching children how to structure their thinking to solve completely new, non-standard problems under time pressure.
